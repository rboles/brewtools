Alcohol By Volume
Calculate alcohol by volume (ABV) more...
There are two common equations for calculating alcohol by volume:
Daniels: (76.08 * (OG-FG) / (1.775-OG)) * (FG / 0.794)
Papazian: (OG - FG) * 131.25
The Papazian equation is most typically used. The Daniels equation is preferred for higher gravity beers. The Papazian equation is the default.
